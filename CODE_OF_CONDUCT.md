# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus critique on code, documentation, behavior, and reproducible evidence.
- Respect privacy and do not expose credentials, logs, or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Disruptive behavior that prevents maintainers or contributors from working.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable action to protect the project. Security reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
